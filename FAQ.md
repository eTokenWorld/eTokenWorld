# FAQ

**Published: 28 March 2026**

## What is eTokenWorld?

eTokenWorld is the human-facing gateway platform powered by QoreMesh OS.

## What is QoreMesh OS?

QoreMesh OS is the foundational infrastructure layer beneath eTokenWorld, directed toward trusted coordination, structured digital participation, governed service environments, interoperable ecosystem growth, and future intelligent and connected-system interaction.

## What is the New World Gateway?

The New World Gateway is the public positioning of eTokenWorld as a trusted entry point into a broader digital environment for participation, services, ecosystem coordination, and connected-system interaction.

## What is User Gateway Access?

User Gateway Access is the participant-facing access layer within eTokenWorld for identity-linked access, service participation, trusted records, and broader ecosystem engagement.

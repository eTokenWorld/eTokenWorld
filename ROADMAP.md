# Roadmap Summary

**Published: 28 March 2026**

## Summary

eTokenWorld is being developed as a human-facing gateway platform powered by QoreMesh OS.

The public roadmap direction includes:

- public gateway foundation
- ecosystem and positioning clarity
- developer awareness
- operator participation pathways
- AI direction
- IoT direction
- future mobility and machine-service direction
- institutional and strategic collaboration pathways

## Current Public Priorities

### Public Foundation
Strengthen the public-facing repository, positioning, and ecosystem materials.

### Builder Readiness
Support understanding for developers, AI builders, IoT developers, and future ecosystem participants.

### Partnership Readiness
Present eTokenWorld in a form suitable for ecosystem collaboration, institutional interest, and strategic dialogue.

### Long-Range Direction
Maintain alignment around trusted digital participation, governed services, intelligent ecosystem growth, and connected real-world coordination.

## Note

This summary is public-safe and directional. It does not disclose confidential implementation detail or protected engineering structure.

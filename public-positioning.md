# Public Positioning

**Published: 28 March 2026**

## Core Positioning

**eTokenWorld, powered by QoreMesh OS, is the New World Gateway for trusted digital participation, governed services, intelligent ecosystem growth, and connected real-world coordination.**

eTokenWorld is the human-facing gateway layer built on top of QoreMesh OS.

QoreMesh OS provides the foundational infrastructure direction for trusted digital coordination, structured participation, governed service environments, interoperable ecosystem growth, and future intelligent and connected-system interaction. eTokenWorld presents that direction in a public-facing form that people, organizations, operators, institutions, developers, and connected infrastructures can understand and use.

## Short Form

eTokenWorld is the human-facing platform powered by QoreMesh OS.

## Expanded Form

eTokenWorld is being developed to bring together digital identity, programmable participation, governed services, trusted records, ecosystem coordination, and future intelligent and connected-system interaction in a unified platform for real-world use.

At a broader level, eTokenWorld is intended to serve as a gateway into a new kind of digital environment where people, ecosystems, institutions, intelligent systems, and connected infrastructure can participate through one coherent public-facing platform rather than through fragmented and disconnected digital silos.

## New World Gateway

eTokenWorld is positioned as the **New World Gateway**.
